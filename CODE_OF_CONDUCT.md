# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1, as its Code of Conduct. The full, authoritative text is published at:

<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for
everyone, and to act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse,
inclusive, and healthy community.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported privately to the maintainers at **support@bymax.one**. All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the reporter's privacy
and security will be respected.

The Contributor Covenant 2.1 standards and enforcement guidelines apply in full;
see the linked text above.
